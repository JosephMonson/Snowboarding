The Winterstick Roundtail

  1. The invention
    • The first snowboard was made in 1965 by bolting 2 skis together
    • Named "The Snurfer"
  2. Slowly Refined
    • Soon the "Winterstick" was developed.
    • A much more refined design that looks as we know it today, with edges and straps.
  3. Recognition
    • Finally, in 1998, Snowboarding was recognized as a real sport by the Olympic Committee at Nagano Japan
